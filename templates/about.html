What is Papersγ ?

  • Comfortable place to discuss scientific articles

    Use it to organise open reading groups, to discuss papers with authors and other readers.

  • List of papers you like

    Here "you" can refer to a group account of a particular research team.
    Group acoount can like (using a ☆-shaped button) a paper to highlight
    the importance of an article for the field.

  • Open source and open data scientific platform

    Please see this page to learn more about how Papersγ's use your data.

By joining the expansion of open science initiatives, Papersγ will grow bigger and stay a convenient place to discuss scientific papers. You have numerous possibilities to contribute: organise a reading group in your team, upload articles, comment and answer, donate time or became a gold sponsor. Just contact me if you wish so.
